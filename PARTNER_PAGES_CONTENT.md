## PARTNER 4: EXACTER
**URL:** `/partners/exacter/`
**Slug:** `exacter`

### Basic Info
- **Company Name:** Exacter
- **Short Description:** Cutting edge reliability technology
- **Tagline:** The Data to Predict. The Power to Prevent.
- **Website:** exacterinc.com

### Full Description
Exacter is an industry leading provider of utility infrastructure preservation technology. The company's primary offering identifies deteriorating equipment emitting pre-failure electrical signatures (arcing, leaking, or tracking) that can be removed from overhead utility infrastructure to prevent outages before they occur.

### How It Works
Exacter vehicles traverse circuit routes at normal speeds, detecting failure signatures from overhead equipment. When detected, GPS coordinates are automatically recorded and uploaded to their server. Field teams then visit confirmed locations to identify specific components emitting pre-failure electrical signatures.

### Deliverables
Reports include:
- Photo of at-risk equipment
- Pole number
- GPS coordinates
- Street location
- Equipment risk description

Reports available in PDF, KMZ, and CSV formats for integration with existing OMS and GIS systems.
